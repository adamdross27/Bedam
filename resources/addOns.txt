Each accommodation type has unique add ons that can be included:

For a house, the add-ons that can be included are:
   - A Pool:		$50 per night
   - A Garage:		$15 per night
   - A Backyard:	$20 per night

For an apartment, the add-ons that can be included are:
   - A Laundry:		$20 per night
   - A Parking Spot:	$50 per night
   - A Balcony:		$30 per night

For a room, the add-ons that can be included are:
   - Warm Towels	$10 per night
   - Private Room	$45 per night
   - Air Conditioning	$20 per night
